The Perfect Start to a Fun and Joyous Season

Wednesday, Dec. 4
5 – 7 p.m.
Grand Salon, University Student Union

It’s the most wonderful time of the year, Matadors!

Come share the joy of the season with your friends at the University Student Union’s Winter Pop Up! With finals right around the corner, the USU would like to gift you with a merry little study break to help you get right into the holiday spirit. You can join us to decorate your very own ornament, indulge in delicious sweet desserts and cozy up with a cup of tasty hot chocolate. Then, keep the fun festivities going by capturing warm memories with friends in our photo booth and skating around our special ice rink in the Grand Salon! There’s no better way to celebrate the season than the USU Winter Pop Up!

Dates and times are subject to change.
For up-to-date information visit csun.edu/src or call (818) 677-5434
