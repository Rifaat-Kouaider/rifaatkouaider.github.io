Dedicated to Student Veterans Here at CSUN!

The Veterans Resource Center of the University Student Union invites you to join us for We ♥ Our Veterans Week—a week-long event dedicated to recognizing our remarkable Student Veterans here at CSUN! Show your gratitude and support for the more than 700 members of our Veteran community and take part in a variety of awesome events all week long including the We ♥ Our Veterans Week Block Party in the Plaza del Sol, the Veterans Resource Center Open House and so much more!

For additional information on We ♥ Our Veterans Week, visit the VRC calendar webpage.
