Thursday, Oct. 17

5 – 8:30 p.m.
Pride Center, Sol Center (2nd Floor)
University Student Union

You’re invited to the Pride Center for an evening of connection and American Sign Language during Deaf Queer Coffee! You’ll be able to enjoy refreshing coffee, tasty snacks and signed poetry performances while immersing yourself in a signing environment with your fellow matadors. All are welcome! Come build a community at Deaf Queer Coffee at the Pride Center… Where you can be proud to be you!

Dates and times are subject to change.
For up-to-date information visit csun.edu/pride or call (818) 677-4355
