Share your spirit of the season!

Tuesday, Dec. 3
10:30 a.m. – Noon
Oasis Wellness Center, University Student Union

Nothing says “Happy Holidays” quite like a handmade note. That’s why the Oasis Wellness Center of the University Student Union is inviting you to express your holiday happiness during December Craft Creations! Get into the spirit of the season by crafting your own heartfelt message with a handmade holiday card for someone special — free to all CSUN students. Showcase your holiday spirit and creativity this season during December Craft Creations at the Oasis Wellness Center… your destination to relax, revive and succeed!

Dates and times are subject to change.
For up-to-date information visit csun.edu/src or call (818) 677-5434
